Khwaja Yunus Ali University (KYAU) is a private university in Enayetpur, Sirajganj, Bangladesh. It was established in 2012 and named after the Sufi saint Khwaja Yunus Ali. KYAU offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines. The university emphasizes both academic Excellence and spiritual values. Its campus is in a peaceful area near the Jamuna River.
